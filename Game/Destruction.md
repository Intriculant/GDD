# Weapon Attack
- Advanced targetting ai that constantly adjusts.
- Angle arms to fire.
- Geometry can block shots.
- Bullets can phase through one infantry unit.

# Area of Effect
- Explosions give knockback.
- Should help reduce packing of units.

# Destruction
- Building rubble.
- Fracture physics.
- Flash fires from explosions.
- Unit ragdoll and persistence.
- Helicopter rudder detatchment.
- Smoky particles in water if explosions went off.
- Destroyed ships/planes persist at the bottom of the ocean.

# Recovery
- Allow rubble to be excavated and reused or turned into cash.
